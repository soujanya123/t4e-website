Sponsors

T4E 2015 is happy to offer three conference packages. The platinum, gold, and silver sponsorship packages allow conference participants to gain maximum brand exposure at the T4E 2015 conference events.

The sponsorship packages offer sponsors a number of branding opportunities. Some of these benefits include recognition as event sponsor, exhibition booths at the conference venue, logo on the workshop bags, and bag inserts among others.

Other Sponsorship Categories

Category A: You could sponsor any or all of the following. Please select.

  • Production of registration packs (Rs. 40,000)
  • Cost of icebreaker reception (Rs. 35,000)
  • Printing of abstract volume (Rs. 30,000)
  • Poster board hire (Rs. 40,000)
  • Hosting expenses for keynote speakers (Rs. 40,000 each)

What you get?

  • Space for your display stand (including kit)
  • Your logo in conference publicity such as abstract volume, correspondence, conference literature, and conference audiovisual system.
  • 50% discount in conference registration for 1 delegate

Category B: You could sponsor the following:

  1. Lecture room hire and audiovisual equipment (Rs. 10,000)

  2. Drinks for poster session (Rs. 15,000)

  3. Contribution to conference dinner (Rs. 15,000)

  4. Local transport (Rs. 10,000)
  5. Any other contribution specifying how you would like it to be used

What you get?

  • Space for your display stand (including kit)
  • Your logo in conference publicity such as abstract volume, correspondence, conference literature, and conference audiovisual system.
  • 50% discount in conference registration for 1 delegate

We expect about 200 delegates at the conference. Many of these will be faculty, educational institutions, researchers, future purchasers of equipment and software such as yours.
