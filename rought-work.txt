	<h2>
	<p >Areas of Focus</p>
	</h2>  
	<p>The areas of focus include, but are not limited to, the following broad topics in the domain  intersecting technology and education:    </p>
		<ul>   
			<li>Adaptive, cognitive and personalized approaches for improvement of education</li>           
			<li>Assessment and evaluation models </li>    <li>Bridging urban-rural gap in education</li>   
			<li> Cognition and learning technologies, Role of cognition in education</li>       
			<li>Curriculum design for technology-enhanced learning</li>     
			<li>Development of technologies to support education</li>   
			<li>e-learning in vernacular languages</li>      
			<li>Game-based learning</li>     
			<li>Growth and impact of the Open Educational Resources movement</li>     
			<li>Hand held device-based learning</li>     
			<li>Impact of social networks on learning</li>      
			<li>Impact studies</li>       
			<li>Infrastructure for large scale deployment of e-learning</li>         
			<li>Innovative use of ICT for content development and classroom use</li>      
			<li>Learning analytics</li>     
			<li>Massively open online learning</li>      
			<li>Pedagogies for remote learning, e-learning, blended learning, and technology-enhanced learning</li>    
			<li>Techniques and standards for learning resources</li>       
			<li>Technology-based learning environments</li>      
			<li>Semantic web technologies in education</li>      
			<li>Smart Learning Environments </li>  
			<li>Socioeconomic factors reducing the impact of technology in education  </li>    
			<li>Virtual/online experimentation and scalable solutions</li>
